Something short about me

I'm Marija Tusek, Photographer and student of Graphic Design.

I'm a 21-year-old Serbian girl living in Malta. Currently I'm studying Bachelor's in Graphic Design and I'm a self-thought Photographer.

When it comes to photography, taking photos of people has always been my passion. When shooting my clients I always like to go with the flow and just have fun, I love to make them feel beautiful, confident and show them that having a photoshoot isn't something they should be scared of. Capturing moments and showing people through artistic eyes is something that makes my soul happy.
